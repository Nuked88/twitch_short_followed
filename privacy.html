Privacy Policy – Twitch Followed Order

The Twitch Followed Order extension does not collect any user data. It exclusively stores the user's custom channel order and the number of channels to display locally, using the chrome.storage.local API. This allows your preferences to persist across browser sessions. No personal information, browsing data, or usage statistics are collected or transmitted to any external server.

What data is stored

The following data is stored locally on your device only:

  • The custom sort order of your followed Twitch channels (list of usernames).
  • The number of channels you have chosen to display in the sidebar.

This data never leaves your browser and is never shared with anyone.

Third-party services

This extension operates entirely on twitch.tv and does not communicate with any third-party service, API, or server on its own.
